Description

The Pitch (Short)

A roguelite plinko-style pinbuilder about escaping the space between heaven & hell. Forge powerful synergies, trigger chaotic screen filling effects and rack up huge scores to secure your resurrection.

The Game (Long)

Grid Grief is a roguelite pinbuilder where players navigate a haunting afterlife by strategically placing pins on an ever-evolving grid. Every placement is a gamble between progression and permanent loss, forcing players to weigh immediate rewards against long-term survival.

As your run goes on, you will uncover more unique pins, perks & secrets and that trigger explosive combos. By combining charms and perks, players can create "game-breaking" synergies that snowball a standard run into a dazzling, rule-bending escape attempt.

Grid Grief is a sinister rogue-lite "pin-builder" set in a haunting afterlife. Described as the biblical lovechild of Clover Pit and Nubby's Number Factory, it injects a high-stakes twist into the genre: your only hope for escape is to build a grid capable of shattering a god.

At its heart, Grid Grief is a dark tale of addiction and escape, where strategic placement determines whether you transcend to glory or remain forever lost in the grief of the grid.

Core Features

  • Rogue-lite Strategy: A high-stakes deckbuilder (pin-builder) where every round could be your last.
  • 100+ Unique Pin Effects: Endless variety in how you influence the grid.
  • Game-Breaking Synergies: Discover perks and charms that allow you to bend the rules and break the game.
  • Meta-Progression: Unlockable run modifiers and permanent progression to aid your escape.
  • "Plinky Plonking" Gameplay: Addictive, tactile mechanics centered around forging a god-destroying grid.
