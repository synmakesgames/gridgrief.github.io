Description

The Pitch (Short)

A roguelite plinko-style pinbuilder about escaping the space between heaven & hell. Forge powerful synergies, trigger chaotic screen filling effects and rack up huge scores to secure your resurrection.

The Game (Long)

Grid Grief is a roguelite pin-builder where players navigate a haunting afterlife by strategically placing pins on an ever-evolving grid. Every placement is a gamble between progression and permanent loss, forcing players to weigh immediate rewards against long-term survival.

As your run goes on, you will uncover unique pins, perks, and secrets that trigger explosive combos. By combining charms and perks, players can create "game-breaking" synergies that snowball a standard run into a dazzling, rule-bending escape attempt.

Your only escape path is to build a grid capable of shattering a god.

Grid Grief combines the plinko style of Peggle with roguelike mechanics, putting a unique pin-building twist on the genre inspired by games like Clover Pit and Nubby's Number Factory.

At its heart, Grid Grief is a dark tale of addiction and escape, where strategic placement determines whether you transcend to glory or remain lost in grief.

Core Features

  • Rogue-lite Strategy: A high-stakes pin-builder where every round could be your last.
  • 100+ Unique Pin Effects: Endless variety in how you influence the outcome of your run
  • Game-Breaking Synergies: Discover perks and pin combinations that allow you to bend the rules and break the game.
  • Meta Progression: Unlockable run modifiers and permanent progression to aid your escape.
  • Plinky Plonking! Addictive, tactile mechanics centered around forging a god-destroying grid.
  • Forge a Path: Build a grid capable of shattering a god and escaping the afterlife.
